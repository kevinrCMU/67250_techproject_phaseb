Membership

Members keep MonoMuse running. Your support funds new exhibitions, artist residencies, and free programming for Pittsburgh students. Plus you get unlimited visits, early access to new shows, and invites to member-only events.

Membership Levels

  • Individual — $75/year: Unlimited admission for one, 10% shop discount, member newsletter
  • Dual — $120/year: Unlimited admission for two, all Individual benefits, plus guest passes
  • Family — $160/year: Unlimited admission for two adults and up to three kids, all Dual benefits
  • Patron — $500/year: All Family benefits, plus exhibition previews, name on donor wall, and invites to annual gala

Donate

Not ready for a membership? One-time donations at any amount go directly toward keeping our doors open and our exhibitions free for school groups across the city. Every dollar helps.

To make a donation or learn more about giving, email us at giving@monomuse.org or stop by the front desk.

About MonoMuse

MonoMuse opened in 2018 in Pittsburgh's Oakland neighborhood with a single idea: make a museum where art and technology aren't separate categories. Founded by a group of Carnegie Mellon alumni, the museum started with one gallery and a borrowed projector. Today we operate three floors of exhibition space, a workshop studio, and a rooftop event venue.

Our mission is to make the relationship between creativity and technology visible, accessible, and worth thinking about. We're not trying to predict the future — we're trying to help people see what's already happening.
