Exhibitions

Here is what is on display right now. Exhibitions rotate throughout the year so there is always something new to see.

Echoes of Tomorrow

This one is all about AI and creativity. Think AI-generated visuals, soundscapes that react to how you move through the room, and digital sculptures that feel almost alive. It raises some interesting questions about what it means to make art when a machine is involved. Worth spending some time with.

Pixel and Clay

A combination of craftsmanship and digital tools. You will see ceramics designed in 3D software, textiles woven by robots, and paintings made through augmented reality. It is a cool look at how traditional art forms are being remixed with modern tech.

The Light Within

Exactly what it sounds like. Large-scale installations, projection-mapped walls, and neon pieces from artists around the world. Some of the rooms respond to sound and movement so the experience changes depending on who is in the space. Great for photos, even better in person.
