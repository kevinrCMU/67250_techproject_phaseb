Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary art and tech museum in the heart of Pittsburgh. We have rotating exhibitions, interactive installations, and events that are worth checking out whether you are into art, technology, or just looking for something different to do on a weekend. Come through with friends, take your time exploring, and see what catches your eye. No pretentious gallery vibes here — just cool things to look at and experience at your own pace.
